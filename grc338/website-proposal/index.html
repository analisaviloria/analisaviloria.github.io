Introduction

Nanang Rosa's Bakery

Nanang Rosa's Bakery is a local, small business Filipino bakery that uses only the freshest and most quality ingredients. Every baked good is made from a recipe passed down multiple generations, starting from Nanang (Mother in Tagalog) Rosa herself!

Target audience

The target audience would be the Filipino and Filipino-American surrounding community, as well as anyone interested in learning more about Filipino culture and trying our food.

Their primary goals by visiting this site are to learn more about the history of Nanang Rosa's Bakery and to view more details (ingredients, price, etc.) about our baked goods.

Our History

Nanang Rosa was born in Tubao, La Union, Philippines on October 2, 1925 and immigrated to the States in 1975. Whether in the Philippines or in the US, Nanang Rosa's #1 priority was to show her love for her family and friends, and she did exactly that by cooking food! Every single item on our menu has been derived from an original recipe made by our very own Nanang Rosa. So what are you waiting for? Kain na! Let's eat!
